Sponsorship Details

Warm welcome to the companies and institutions wishing to sponsor this Conference. Sponsor's assistance will be mainly utilised to support students, academicians/scientists/researchers and invitees participation in the conference. For all sponsors, the general advantages are the following:

  • The sponsor will be provided a space for displaying their advertisements during the seminar.
  • A sponsor list will be included in the seminar program and abstract book (souvenir).
